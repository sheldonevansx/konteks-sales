## Notes for ActiveCampaign Setup

**Automation flow:**
1. Lead captured via guide.html, exit-popup.js, or quiz.html
2. Email 1 sends immediately (Day 0)
3. Email 2 sends 2 days later (Day 2)
4. Email 3 sends 2 days after Email 2 (Day 4)
5. Email 4 sends 3 days after Email 3 (Day 7)
6. Email 5 sends 3 days after Email 4 (Day 10)
7. Email 6 sends 4 days after Email 5 (Day 14)

**Tagging:**
- Source tag: `free-guide`, `exit-popup`, or `quiz`
- Engagement tags: track opens, clicks
- Conversion tag: if they purchase before sequence ends, stop sending

**Testing:**
- A/B test subject lines on first 100 leads
- Track conversion rate per email
- Optimize based on which emails drive the most clicks/purchases
