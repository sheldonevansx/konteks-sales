## 01. THE DEBUNK (ad-01-debunk.html)
**Scroll-Stop: 7/10** — Bold contrarian statement stops scrolling, but lacks visual punch  
**Emotional Hit: 6/10** — Challenges belief without deep pain activation

**Uniqueness: 6/10** — Contrarian hooks are everywhere in edu-marketing

# RECOMMENDATIONS

**Launch with:** 09, 10, 02, 13, 14 (top 5)  
**Test next:** 05, 06, 08 (proven formats, solid execution)  
**Rework before using:** 03, 04, 12 (good concepts, weak visuals)

**General observations:**
- Dark backgrounds + orange accents consistently outperform light backgrounds
- Specificity wins (3AM, 6 red bulls, sun path emojis) over generic pain
- Questions and stats have lower emotional impact than scenarios
- Dan's credentials are underutilized across the board
- The best ads trigger instant recognition ("I've lived this exact moment")

**What's missing:**
- Student testimonial/social proof (you have 363 students — use them)
- Dan's face (people buy from people, not brands)
- Urgency/scarcity (limited spots, cohort starting, etc.)
- Specific outcome promise ("Get your concept approved in 48h")
