# Meta Ad Library Research - Architecture Education Competitors
**Research Date:** March 23, 2026  
**Target Audience:** Architecture students, architecture education adjacent audiences  
**Research Method:** Facebook Ad Library search + competitor website analysis

---

## Executive Summary

**Key Finding:** **Archademia dominates Meta advertising** for architecture education, running 50+ ads continuously from Sep 2025-Mar 2026. They are the only direct architecture course competitor actively spending significant budget on Facebook/Instagram ads.

**Major Gap:** No competitors are targeting architecture *students* specifically with Meta ads. Most focus on professionals, BIM/software certifications, or adjacent civil engineering audiences.

**Opportunity for Konteks Kourse:** Be the ONLY player targeting architecture students with Meta ads focused on design education (not software training).

## 5. CTA Strategies

| CTA | Frequency | Conversion Intent |
|-----|-----------|-------------------|
| Learn More | HIGH (Archademia dominant) | Low-friction, discovery |
| Apply Now | MEDIUM (BIM courses) | Higher intent |
| Sign Up | MEDIUM | Registration required |
| Send Message | LOW | 1-on-1 sales conversation |
| Enroll Now | LOW | Direct purchase |

**Key Insight:** "Learn More" dominates because it lowers friction for cold traffic. Most advertisers are driving to landing pages, not direct checkout.
