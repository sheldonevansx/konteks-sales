## KEY INSIGHTS FOR KONTEKS KOURSE POSITIONING

### Market Segments Identified:

1. **ARE/Licensure Exam Prep** (Amber Book, Black Spectacles, Young Architect, Designer Hacks)
   - High-ticket or recurring revenue models
   - B2C and B2B (firm licenses)
   - Very specific, outcome-driven value prop

2. **Software/Tech Training** (Think Parametric, The SketchUp Essentials, Show it Better)
   - Subscription or certification models
   - Focus on staying current with evolving tech
   - Taught by industry practitioners, not academics

3. **Design Process & Portfolio** (Archisoup, First In Architecture)
   - Productized solutions to common student pain points
   - Lower-ticket digital products ($39-$300 range)
   - Content marketing + email list nurture strategy

4. **Rendering & Visualization** (Show it Better, Think Parametric AI courses)
   - Mix of free YouTube content + paid courses
   - Often includes AI tools (Midjourney, etc.)
   - Services (commissions) + education hybrid model

### Common Success Patterns:

✅ **Free Content → Paid Course Funnel** (almost all use this)  
✅ **Personal Brand/Face** (Justin Geis, Steven Rubio, Eric Reinholdt)  
✅ **Outcome-Based Positioning** ("Get licensed", "Master SketchUp", "Build portfolio that gets interviews")  
✅ **Community/Support** (Young Architect's "until you're licensed", Think Parametric's membership)  
✅ **Multiple Price Points** (Young Architect: $129/month vs. $1,300 bootcamp)  
✅ **Productized Solutions** (kits, templates, toolkits - not just "courses")  
✅ **Subscription Models** (Think Parametric, Black Spectacles for firms)  

### Gaps & Opportunities:

1. **No one is doing "anti-academic" positioning like Dan** - All competitors are earnest/traditional in tone
2. **Pop culture references are RARE** - This could be a differentiator for Konteks
3. **Most are software-focused OR exam-focused** - Few are purely about *design thinking and concept development* the way Konteks could be
4. **YouTube is THE lead gen channel** - Not Instagram (yet). Dan's Instagram presence could be unique advantage
5. **Pricing sweet spots:** $25-$50 for entry courses, $129-$300 for main products, $1,000-$1,500 for bootcamps/certifications
